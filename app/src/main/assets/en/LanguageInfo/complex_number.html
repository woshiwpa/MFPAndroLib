MFP language complex number support:

    A complex number in MFP is written as a + b * i or a + bi, where a and b are real numbers (can be both positive or negative). If a is 0, then the complex number is written as b*i or bi. Note that bi is right while b i is wrong according to MFP because it believe b and i are two different variables because they are seperated by a blank character.
